## Create Your First Dataset

Follow these steps to import data and start transforming it with Rowbase.

### Step 1: Create an Organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [app.rowbase.com](https://app.rowbase.com) and create your account using email or Google SSO.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an organization">
    After signing in, you'll be prompted to create your first organization. This is where all your projects and datasets will live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team (optional)">
    Navigate to **Settings â†’ Members** to invite teammates to collaborate on your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Create a Project

Projects help you organize related datasets together.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    From your organization dashboard, click the **New Project** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your project">
    Give your project a descriptive name like "Q1 Sales Analysis" or "Customer Data Cleanup".
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Import Your Data

1. Click **Upload** in your project
2. Drag and drop your file or click to browse
3. Supported formats: CSV, Excel (.xlsx, .xls), JSON
4. Rowbase automatically detects column types and headers
5. Review the preview and click **Import**

<Tip>
You can upload multiple files at once. Each file becomes a separate dataset.
</Tip>

### Step 4: Transform Your Data

Once your data is imported, you can apply operations to clean and transform it.

<CardGroup cols="2">
