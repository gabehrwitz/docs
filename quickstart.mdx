## Create Your First Dataset

Follow these steps to import data and start transforming it with Rowbase.

### Step 1: Create an Organization

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [app.rowbase.com](https://app.rowbase.com) and create your account using email or Google SSO.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an organization">
    After signing in, you'll be prompted to create your first organization. This is where all your projects and datasets will live.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team (optional)">
    Navigate to **Settings â†’ Members** to invite teammates to collaborate on your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Create a Project

Projects help you organize related datasets together.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    From your organization dashboard, click the **New Project** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your project">
    Give your project a descriptive name like "Q1 Sales Analysis" or "Customer Data Cleanup".
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Import Your Data

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CSV Upload">
    1. Click **Upload** in your project
    2. Drag and drop your CSV file or click to browse
    3. Rowbase automatically detects column types and headers
    4. Review the preview and click **Import**
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Paste Data">
    1. Click **Paste** in your project
    2. Copy data from Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet
    3. Paste directly into Rowbase
    4. Review and confirm the import
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API">
    ```bash
    curl -X POST https://api.rowbase.com/v1/datasets \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
      -d '{
        "name": "My Dataset",
        "project_id": "proj_xxx",
        "data": [
          {"name": "Alice", "email": "alice@example.com"},
          {"name": "Bob", "email": "bob@example.com"}
        ]
      }'
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 4: Transform Your Data

Once your data is imported, you can apply operations to clean and transform it.

<CardGroup cols="2">
  <Card title="Filter Rows" icon="filter">
    Remove rows that don't match specific criteria
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sort Data" icon="arrow-down-a-z">
    Order your data by any column
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remove Duplicates" icon="clone">
    Deduplicate rows based on key columns
  </Card>
  <Card title="Add Formulas" icon="function">
    Create computed columns with spreadsheet-like formulas
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
