## What is Rowbase?

Rowbase is a collaborative data platform that helps teams clean, transform, and manage their data without writing code. Whether you're preparing data for analysis, building data pipelines, or managing datasets across your organization, Rowbase makes it simple.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Datasets" icon="table">
    Create and manage structured datasets with automatic type detection and schema validation. Import from CSV, Excel, or JSON files.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Operations" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Transform your data with 40+ operations including filtering, renaming, parsing, and AI-powered transformations. Chain operations together to build reusable pipelines.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Data Quality" icon="shield-check">
    Compare changes with diff view, edit cells inline, profile column statistics, and quarantine problematic rows for review.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI Assistant" icon="robot">
    Chat with your data using natural language, get AI-suggested operations, and use AI-powered transformations to categorize and enrich data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Formulas" icon="function">
    Create computed columns using familiar spreadsheet-like formulas. Reference other columns, use built-in functions, and build complex calculations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Collaboration" icon="users">
    Share datasets with your team and control access with granular permissions at the organization and project level.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why Teams Choose Rowbase

- **No-code data transformation** - Clean and transform data without writing scripts
- **AI-powered** - Use AI to categorize, extract, and enrich your data
- **Inline editing** - Edit cells directly with automatic correction tracking
- **Data quality tools** - Diff view, profiling, and quarantine for problematic rows
- **Real-time collaboration** - Work together on datasets with your team
