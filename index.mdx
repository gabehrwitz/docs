## What is Rowbase?

Rowbase is a collaborative data platform that helps teams clean, transform, and manage their data without writing code. Whether you're preparing data for analysis, building data pipelines, or managing datasets across your organization, Rowbase makes it simple.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Datasets" icon="table">
    Create and manage structured datasets with automatic type detection, schema validation, and version history. Import from CSV, Excel, or connect directly to your data sources.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Operations" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Transform your data with powerful operations like filtering, sorting, deduplication, and more. Chain operations together to build reusable data pipelines.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Formulas" icon="function">
    Create computed columns using familiar spreadsheet-like formulas. Reference other columns, use built-in functions, and build complex calculations.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Collaboration" icon="users">
    Share datasets with your team, control access with granular permissions, and track changes with full audit history.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Why Teams Choose Rowbase

- **No-code data transformation** - Clean and transform data without writing scripts
- **Version control** - Track every change with automatic versioning
- **Real-time collaboration** - Work together on datasets with your team
- **API-first** - Integrate with your existing tools and workflows
- **Enterprise-ready** - SOC 2 compliant with SSO and advanced security features
